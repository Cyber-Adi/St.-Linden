gaga ball pit

Was added in 2012

Gaga is a fast paced, high energy sport played in an octagonal pit. The more players the better! Dubbed a kinder gentler version of dodge ball, the game is played with a soft foam ball, and combines the skills of dodging, striking, running, and jumping, while trying to hit opponents with a ball below the knees.

  • New School Canteen

    The school canteen plays a vital role in promoting healthy foods and creating a school culture of healthy eating. Food preferences are developed in childhood. By promoting and encouraging healthier food choices during childhood, it is anticipated that healthy eating habits will be carried through to adulthood and may go some way to help prevent chronic disease later in life.

  • New Virtual Academy in Response to Pandemic

    Going to school can be daunting for many teens. Peer pressure and social drama can be overwhelming. When teens feel anxious about going to school, the quality of their learning can be compromised. No child should have to deal with bullying, but unfortunately, bullying is prevalent today. In online school, your child can complete their school-work in a learning environment that you control, where students are free from distractions and feel safe. You will see your child regain and build self-confidence and independence. My child actually started smiling again!
